    Agent Harness: Understanding the Architecture Behind AI Agents

    Let's talk about what an "agent harness" actually means in the world of artificial intelligence.

    To understand this, let's start with the word harness itself. A harness is something that lets you control energy and direct it toward a goal. In simple terms, it is not the energy source, but the structure that makes the energy useful.

    That exact philosophy applies to modern AI.

    An agent harness is the software architecture built around a GenAI model to control how it plans tasks, remembers information, and uses tools over long periods of time. The model provides intelligence, but the harness turns that intelligence into purposeful action. Without this structure, you have intelligence without direction. With it, you have a capable system that can achieve meaningful goals. ğŸ’¡
